Meal Planning

Advice on planning balanced meals.

Healthy Recipes

1. Breakfast

  • Avocado Toast: Whole-grain bread with smashed avocado, salt, pepper, and a poached egg.
  • Chia Seed Pudding: Chia seeds soaked in almond milk, sweetened with honey, topped with berries.
  • Vegetable Omelette: Eggs with diced tomatoes, spinach, and onions cooked in a non-stick skillet.

2. Lunch

  • Quinoa Black Bean Salad: Quinoa, black beans, diced peppers, corn, and lime dressing.
  • Grilled Chicken Bowl: Grilled chicken on mixed greens with cherry tomatoes and a light vinaigrette.
  • Lentil Soup: Lentils cooked with carrots, celery, onion, and vegetable broth.

3. Dinner

  • Baked Salmon: Salmon fillets seasoned with lemon and garlic, baked with asparagus.
  • Stuffed Bell Peppers: Bell peppers filled with brown rice, ground turkey, and spices.
  • Zucchini Noodles: Spiralized zucchini tossed with pesto, cherry tomatoes, and grilled shrimp.

4. Snacks

  • Greek Yogurt with Honey: Protein-packed yogurt topped with honey and walnuts.
  • Energy Bites: Oats, nut butter, honey, and chocolate chips rolled into balls.
  • Apple Slices with Almond Butter: Simple and satisfying.

Healthy Eating Habits

  • Plan Ahead: Meal prep weekly to save time and ensure healthy choices.
  • Mindful Eating: Eat slowly and savor each bite to prevent overeating.
  • Balanced Meals: Include protein, healthy fats, and fiber-rich carbs in every meal.
  • Portion Control: Be mindful of serving sizes and hunger cues.
  • Stay Hydrated: Drink plenty of water and include hydrating foods.

Meal Planning Tips

  • Batch Cooking: Prepare larger quantities of meals for the week.
  • Theme Nights: Use themed dinner nights for easier planning.
  • Flexible Plans: Allow adjustments for cravings while keeping healthy options.
  • Seasonal Ingredients: Use seasonal fruits and vegetables for better flavor.
  • Healthy Snacks: Incorporate healthy snacks to avoid unhealthy choices.
